Privacy Policy

At Shared Harmony, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our roommate-matching services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to personalize your experience and improve our services, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email, age, gender, photos, and preferences submitted during registration or profile updates.
  • Match & Interaction Data: Roommate preferences, profile views, messages, and user interactions within the app.
  • Location Data: General location to help suggest nearby matches (with your consent).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage patterns collected via cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Suggest compatible roommate matches
  • Enable communication between users
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud
  • Send updates, notifications, or important changes related to your account

3. Sharing Your Information

We only share your information when necessary and with trusted parties:

  • Other Users: Limited profile details (e.g., name, age, interests) are visible to matched users.
  • Service Providers: We may use third-party tools for hosting, analytics, or messaging.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our users and platform.

4. Your Rights & Choices

You have control over your data. You can:

  • Access or update your profile at any time
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Delete your account and associated information
  • Manage notification preferences

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we’ll remove your personal data within 30 days unless required otherwise by law.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encryption and secure servers. However, no method is entirely foolproof, so we recommend using strong passwords and keeping your credentials confidential.

7. Children’s Privacy

Shared Harmony is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy. We’ll notify you of significant changes via email or in-app alerts. Continued use of the app after changes implies acceptance.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact us at shared.harmonydev@gmail.com.
